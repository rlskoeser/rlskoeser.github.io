<p>The following are some notes from the Fedora/Dspace repositories webinar heald by Sun.</p><p>Basically Sun is offering a project called DuraSpace which is designed to enable Fedora/Dspace to operate with the "cloud" out there on the web.  Primarily they seem to mean interaction between the repository and services like Google Docs, Flickr and things like that.  The push from the repository to the cloud seems to be free open source, but the whole package is a charge project.</p><p>This is a new project in the "pilot partners" stage, they will do a beta release sometime at the end of summer or beginning of fall.  Service launch is slated for the end of the year.</p><p>Doesn't seem to be a project page on the web yet but there will probably be by the time the pilot goes into production.</p>
