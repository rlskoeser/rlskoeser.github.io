<p>Development builds of Google Chrome are available for Debian-based Linux distributions on i386 and amd64 and for Mac OS X 10.5 on Intel. This news is probably less exciting for Mac users, who have had a usable Webkit-based browser for some time, but for Linux users this should be a&nbsp;significant upgrade over Midori, Epiphany-Webkit, etc.</p><p>Please see the&nbsp;<a href="http://dev.chromium.org/getting-involved/dev-channel">Chromium Early Access Release Channels</a>&nbsp;page for more information.</p><p>I've installed the i386 and amd64 .deb packages on Ubuntu 9.04 and they appear to work just fine, aside from a lot of missing configuration options and excessive skepticism of SSL certificates.</p><p>&nbsp;</p>
