<p>Great Entry on using <a href="http://ericholscher.com/blog/2009/jun/11/migrating-test-fixtures-using-south/" target="_blank">South to migrate test fixtures</a> as well over on Eric's Blog.</p><p>It's also a great example of just setting up and using South in general.</p><blockquote><p><em>Migrating test fixtures is one of the biggest pains of testing. If you
create your tests too early, then change your schema, you have to go
back and touch all your old test fixtures. This discourages people from
writing tests until their app is relatively 'stable'. As we all know,
this may never happen :) This solves half of the problem, the part
where you have to manually change a bunch of fixtures to reflect
changes in your schema or data.
</em></p></blockquote>
