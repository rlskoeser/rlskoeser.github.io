<p>The Drupal Bibliography module (http://drupal.org/project/biblio) provides COinS support for content hosted in Drupal. This offers admin-users a very simple way of embedding citation metadata into their content. It also can help catalogers by providing basic metadata to refer to at time of publication. In addition to Zotero using CoINS, other sites also make use of it, including Citebase, Wikipedia,
WorldCat,
and CiteULike. </p><p>By the way the Zotero team built a WordPress plugin that embeds a
COinS tag in each blog post:&nbsp; (<a href="http://www.zotero.org/support/dev/wordpress%29" rel="nofollow">http://www.zotero.org/support/dev/wordpress)</a>.
</p>
