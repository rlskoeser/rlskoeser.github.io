<p>JISC and Microsoft are proud to announce this year's RepoChallenge at Open Repositories 2009.<br /><br />This year the competition is once again requesting prototypes (paper or coded) that "show us the future of repositories!"<br /><br />There will be a developer lounge on both days of the main conference if you want a place to come and pitch or build your idea.  To enter this year's competition you must submit a five minute screencast video of your prototype by Tuesday May 19th at 3pm.<br />The top two winning prototypes will be given a trip to the conference of their choosing in either the US or UK. <br /><br />For further details and rules please see the RepoChallenge web page: https://or09.library.gatech.edu/challenge.php<br /><br />To get specific questions answered please twitter your question with hashtag "#repochallenge" and the organisers will get right back to you :)<br /><br />==================================================================<br /><br />Your OR09 Host Committee</p>
