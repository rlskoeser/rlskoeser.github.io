<p>http://repinf.pbwiki.com</p><p>This wiki is for those interested in:</p>
<p>1. developing coordinated action plans for specific areas of repository development</p>
<p>2. pursuing those plans</p>
<p>3. coordinating that activity internationally</p><h3>The plans</h3>
<p>The current crop of action plans are around four areas.</p>
<p> - <a href="http://repinf.pbwiki.com/Citation-services">Citation services</a> - that is, making citation data more easily available from OA papers</p>
<p> - '<a href="http://repinf.pbwiki.com/Repository-handshake">Repository handshake</a>' - that is, ways for repositories to ease the deposit process in various scenarios</p>
<p> - <a href="http://repinf.pbwiki.com/Interoperable-identification-infrastructure">Interoperable identification infrastructure</a> - that is, can named entities be unambiguously identified on the web?</p><p>
 - International Repositories Organisation - how can repositories and their teams work better together.</p><p> </p>
