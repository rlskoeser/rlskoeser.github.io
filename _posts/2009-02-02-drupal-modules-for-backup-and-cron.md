<p>These two Drupal Modules may be of interest for us as we go forward.</p><p><a href="http://drupal.org/project/backup_migrate" target="_blank">Backup and Migrate</a> - As it says, migrates between sites and backs them up.  </p><p><a href="http://drupal.org/project/poormanscron" target="_blank">Poormanscron</a> - Adds a hook to run cron.php every hour on a pageload.</p><p>I ran across these on the <a href="http://drupalmodules.com/top-rated" target="_blank">Drupal Modules Site</a> which is a great contact and review place for various Drupal Modules.</p>
