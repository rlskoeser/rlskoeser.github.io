<p>The <a href="http://gde.emory.edu/smallpox/">GDE/Smallpox site</a> has been updated with a minor release, with the following features:</p><ul><li>All videos have been converted with the "faststart" utility so users can begin playing the video immediately instead of waiting for the whole video to download</li><li>Metadata entry form has been converted from XForms to a more normal html webform, using Dojo for interactive components<ul><li>now includes a complete country list with an auto-suggest interface</li><li>better validation, record cannot be marked as complete until required fields are filled in and nothing is invalid</li></ul></li><li>New or updated transcript documents can be uploaded to audio and video records</li></ul>
